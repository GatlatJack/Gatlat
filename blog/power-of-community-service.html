The Power of Community Service

How volunteering at Resilience Montreal has shaped my perspective on giving back to the community
Community Volunteering Personal

I Help to prepare and serve meals, organize and distribute clothing,hygiene products while keeping the environment clean and safe

Volunteering at Resilience Montreal has been a transformative experience for me. It has not only allowed me to give back to the community but has also provided me with invaluable lessons in empathy, teamwork, and the importance of social responsibility.

Through my work, I have seen firsthand the impact that community service can have on individuals and families in need. It has reinforced my belief that even small acts of kindness can make a significant difference in someone's life.

I encourage everyone to find ways to get involved in their local communities, whether through volunteering, donating, or simply being a supportive neighbor. Together, we can create a more compassionate and connected world.

Gatlat Deng Bum

Computer Science Student at McGill University
